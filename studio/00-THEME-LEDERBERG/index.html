Cinema's Representation of Diversity

My theme is on contemporary cinema,
specifically on the representation of
diversity in film. I will be examining
whether there has a been a change
and whether or not more work needs
to be done on telling different types
of stories in film.
Hailing from Providence, Rhode Island,
Eli Lederberg is a rising sophomore currently
at Parsons School of Design, where he
is majoring in Communication Design. He got
his first taste of Graphic Design as an intern
Nail Communications, a boutique advertising agency.
He enjoys working with Photoshop, Illustrator,
and Indesign and using them to create new and original pieces.
