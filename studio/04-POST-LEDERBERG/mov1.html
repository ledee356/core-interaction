Call Me By Your Name

Based on the 2007 novel, Call Me By Your Name written by André Aciman, the film is centered around a young 17 year old named Elio growing up in Northern Italy in 1983 and is a masterful love story. Elio’s world is upended when he falls in love with his father’s 24 graduate student Oliver who happens to be staying with them for the entire summer. The film follows the two of them as they fall in love and captures their shared experience that is equally filled with passion and torment. The film is directed by Luca Guadagnino and is also nominated for Best Adapted Screenplay, Best Actor, and Best Original Song.
